The Problem

Empty storefronts drain the vibrancy of a community.

  • Vacant spaces signal decline to potential tenants and visitors
  • Artists need affordable studio space but rent is a barrier
  • Communities lose foot traffic and energy

The Solution

OpenStudios fills vacant storefronts with practicing local artists, and at no cost to the landlord.

Landlords give space

  • Animated storefront
  • Zero financial obligation

Artists get studios

  • Free studio space
  • Community visibility

For Landlords

  • Liability insurance provided
  • Utility expenses covered if required
  • Social media promotion and visibility
  • Demonstrates space's potential to future tenants
  • No financial obligation

Terminate with agreed-upon notice (typically 30 days) when the space rents.

For Artists

  • Free studio space (typically 3-4 months)
  • Any medium welcome
  • No degree or credentials required
  • Community visibility and foot traffic
  • Opportunity to sell work directly

Artists maintain active practice (10+ hrs/week) and participate in Final Fridays.

How It Works

  1. Apply — Artists and landlords apply on our website
  2. Selection — Local committee reviews and matches
  3. Setup — Artist moves in, we promote the launch
  4. Residency — 3-4 months of studio practice and community engagement
  5. Transition — Space rented? Success. Still vacant? May extend.

We Handle Everything

  • Artist selection and vetting
  • Liability insurance
  • Utilities (if required)
  • Contracts and legal agreements
  • Promotion and social media
  • Event coordination
